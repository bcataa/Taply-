Premium

Extra perks when you're ready to level up

Taply is free and you can save and edit your profile anytime. Premium is optional and adds a few nice extras.

What you get with Premium

  • Custom branding – Your link-in-bio page can show your name or brand instead of "Taply" in the footer.
  • Cleaner link – Option to hide the "Made with Taply" line so your page looks fully yours.
  • Same everything else – Unlimited links, themes, analytics, and saving work exactly the same on the free plan.

Free plan

You get one main link (your username). You can change the display name anytime. All features for building and editing your page are available. Saving your profile is never restricted.
