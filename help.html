What Taply does & how to use it

Taply is a free link-in-bio service. You get one shareable link (e.g. taply.ro/your-username) that you can add to Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, or anywhere. When people tap it, they see your profile with a photo, bio, social links, and all the links you add.

Links

Add as many links as you want: your store, blog, music, portfolio, contact form, etc. Each link has a title and URL. You can reorder them by dragging. Taply also supports short links (e.g. taply.ro/go/username/slug) for cleaner URLs in bios.

Design

Choose a theme (Sunset, Midnight, Ivory, Forest, Minimal, Leather, Purple, Grid) to style your page. You can set a custom background image. Links and buttons adapt to the theme.

Profile

Set your display name, bio, and profile photo. Add social handles (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, etc.) so visitors can follow you on other platforms.

Audience

Manage which social platforms appear on your profile. Pick up to five. You can change this anytime.

Insights

See how many people visited your profile and how many times each link was clicked. Simple analytics to understand what works.

Link & QR

Copy your profile URL to share anywhere. Generate a QR code for your profile so people can scan it (e.g. on flyers, business cards).

Your profile link

After signing up, you choose a username. Your public page lives at taply.ro/your-username. Share that link in your bio, posts, or QR code.
