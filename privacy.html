Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Taply ("we") is a free link-in-bio service. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data we collect

  • Account: email address, password (stored hashed), and username you choose.
  • Profile: display name, bio, profile photo, links, social handles, and design choices (theme, background image) that you add to your public page.
  • Usage: we may store basic click counts on your links to show you simple analytics.

How we use it

We use your data to provide the service: to let you log in, to show your public profile at your link (e.g. taply.ro/your-username), and to display analytics in your dashboard. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Where data is stored

Account and profile data are stored on our servers and, if configured, in Supabase (cloud database). Profile content you publish is visible to anyone who visits your public link.

Cookies

We use session cookies (or similar) to keep you logged in. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

Your rights

You can update or delete your profile content anytime in the dashboard. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at the email or contact method provided on the Taply website (e.g. on the landing or contact page).
